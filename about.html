About

Firstly, thank you for downloading Spy Watch!

What is Spy Watch?
Spy Watch is a privacy extension that aims to create transparency in third party internet tracking. Our hope is that further transparency can change the way online tracking is discussed at the policy level.

How do I use Spy Watch?
After you've downloaded/installed Spy Watch, continue to browse normally. When you visit a page, click the spy icon (in your toolbar) and you'll see a popup with a list of sites. These sites are tracking you, and currently know you're visiting that page. Clicking on the blue icon next to the sites in the list will take you to the CookieCert page for that site. CookieCert provides more information on the cookies left by certain tracking sites. Please keep in mind however, that not all tracking sites will have entries on CookieCert. Clicking on a site will allow you to see the other pages this site knows you've visited. Clicking on "All tracking sites" will allow you to see every site that has ever tracked you.

What happens with the data generated by Spy Watch?
Nothing! All user data is stored locally in the browser. In fact, clicking on "Clear Data" will clear all of Spy Watch's data. The creator of this extension cannot access your data.

How do I stop these sites from tracking me?
Unfortunately, Spy Watch only informs users of trackers and cannot block them. However, there are many chrome extensions that do block trackers! If you're interested, check out DoNotTrackMe or Ghostery.
